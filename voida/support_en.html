FAQ

Where are my photos saved?

They are saved to a "Voida" album created inside the iOS Photos app. All photos are processed on your device and are never sent to any external server.

Are my photos ever sent over the internet?

No. All image processing — including depth analysis, bokeh generation, and filter (LUT) application — happens entirely on your iPhone. For details, see our Privacy Policy.

How do I restore my purchase?

After switching devices or reinstalling, tap "Restore Purchase" on the in-app purchase screen. You need to be signed in with the same Apple Account you used at the time of purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. You can cancel as follows:

  1. Open "Settings" on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Voida" and tap "Cancel Subscription"

After canceling, you can keep using the app until the end of the current billing period.
