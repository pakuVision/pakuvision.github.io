KISSA

Privacy Policy

Effective: July 18, 2026　／　Operator: pakuvision

KISSA is a photo-editing app that finishes everyday photos with the atmosphere of film. Every photo you edit is processed entirely on your device. Your photos are never sent to our servers or to any third party.

01Basic Policy

pakuvision (the "Operator") sets out this privacy policy regarding the handling of user information in the iOS app "KISSA" (the "App"). The Operator handles only the minimum information necessary to provide the App and does not collect any other information.

02Information We Collect

The information the App exchanges with external services is limited to the following two categories. Neither identifies you as an individual.

(1) Purchase Processing Information

Apple Inc. (App Store / StoreKit) ／ RevenueCat, Inc.

Receipt information and an anonymous app user ID used to process and restore one-time purchases made on the App Store. Used to verify and restore your purchase status. The Operator never obtains your name or credit card information.

(2) Diagnostic & Usage Information

Google LLC (Firebase Crashlytics / Firebase Analytics / Cloud Firestore)

To improve the App's stability and quality, we collect anonymous crash reports, along with anonymous usage events such as which filters and presets are used and their settings. This data is not tied to information that identifies you as an individual.

03Information We Do Not Collect

The following information is never sent externally and is processed entirely on your device.

  • Photos in your photo library (all image processing — filters, bokeh, grain, and so on — is completed on your device)
  • Your edits and presets (stored only on your device)
  • Location, contacts, name, email address, and other personal information

04Purpose of Use

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes.

  • Processing, verifying, and restoring one-time purchases
  • Improving the App's stability and quality, and understanding how features are used

05Provision to Third Parties

We use the services of the following providers located in a foreign country (the United States) for purchase processing and diagnostics, and information is processed on their servers. We do not provide information to any third party other than these.

Apple Inc. Provider of the App Store / StoreKit (purchase processing). Privacy Policy
RevenueCat, Inc. Provider of the purchase management platform. Privacy Policy
Google LLC Provider of Firebase Crashlytics / Firebase Analytics / Cloud Firestore. Privacy Policy

06About Tracking

The App does not obtain the advertising identifier (IDFA) and does not track you across other companies' apps or websites in any way. There are no advertisements.

07Retention and Deletion

Data such as your edits and presets is stored only on your device and is erased the moment you delete the App. No user data remains on the Operator's servers.

08Changes to This Policy

This policy may be revised in response to changes in laws or features. If there are significant changes, we will announce them on this page.
