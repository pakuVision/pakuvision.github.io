Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my edited photos saved?

When you tap SAVE, the photo is saved to "Recents" in the iOS Photos app. Your original photo stays as it is and is never overwritten.

Are my photos ever sent over the internet?

No. All image processing — filters (LUTs), bokeh generation, grain, and more — is completed entirely inside your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is KISSA Pro a subscription?

No, it's a one-time purchase. A single purchase gives you every feature forever, with no expiration. There are no monthly or annual recurring charges.

How do I restore my purchase?

After changing devices or reinstalling, tap "Restore Purchase" on the settings screen inside the app. You must be signed in with the same Apple Account you used at the time of purchase.

How do I save in high quality?

Turn on "High Quality Export" on the settings screen to save at the original photo's resolution (a Pro feature). When off, photos are saved at the same resolution shown on screen.
