Privacy Policy

Your records are yours alone.

ilgi is designed with your privacy as the top priority. This policy explains what information the app handles.

Everything you write — your journal entries, moods, and habits — is stored only on your device. None of this content is ever sent to external servers.

Information Stored on Your Device

The following is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally.

  • Journal entries
  • Added images
  • Mood records
  • Habit tracker data

Information Collected to Improve the App

To improve the quality and stability of the app, we collect the following anonymous information. It does not include any personally identifiable content such as your journal entries.

  • Device identifier (to understand usage)
  • App usage data (Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

Purpose of Use

  • Improving service quality
  • Improving app stability
  • Improving user experience

Third-Party Provision

The anonymous information above is processed through the following service.

  • Firebase (Google LLC): app analytics and crash reporting

We do not provide your information to any other third parties.

Retention Period

  • Records on your device are kept until you delete the app.
  • We will delete data promptly upon your request.
