Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your entries are stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. Your records are yours alone.

Is there a cost to use the app?

All core features — journaling, mood tracking, and the habit tracker — are free to use. There are no ads.

Can I transfer my data to a new device?

Since your entries are stored on your device, please use your device's backup feature to transfer them.
